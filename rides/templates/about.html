So, what exactly is HOVRiders?
Every year, millions of people travel to attend events far and wide, leaving their typically modes of transportation at home. To get around these conferences, these attendees often rent cars or pay for taxis such as Ubers and Lyfts individually, wasting money, time, and overall economic efficiency. HOVRiders was built to combat this problem. Going somewhere unfamiliar? Find a friend to travel with and split the costs! Want to connect to similar conference-goers? Enter your travel information and get paired! It's that simple. Whether you're a solo-hiker, big-time CEO, or the average commuter, HOVRiders has a home for you.
